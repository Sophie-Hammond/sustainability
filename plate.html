How to Make Your Diet More Environmentally Sustainable

Submit a tip:







The production of animals is responsible for over 30% of greenhouse gas emmissions. It is also associated with eutrophication, desertification, reduced water availability and deforestation of the world's rainforests. By reducing the amount of meat, especially beef, in our diet these effects can be reduced.



One third of food produced is wasted. Through buying less food, using surplus food for new meals and recyling food waste, your contribution to global warming can be reduced and you can save money!



Buy locally grown food or food from the UK! This will reduce food miles i.e. the distance that the food has travelled either via planes, boats or lorries; and therefore reduce carbon output. Buying locally grown food will also support businesses and farmers in your community!
